Tender Writing

Government tendering processes are becoming more complex, accountable and more competitive. As a result, tender writing can become very time consuming.

We can assist by providing information on how you can write your own tender and how to look for tender opportunities. You need to know what policies and business practices have an impact on your ability to be able to apply for a tender. Information relates to WA but has relevance in other states with similar legislation.

We recognise that starting and growing a business is very challenging and time consuming and will soon be offering you accredited tendering specialist services.

Personal and Business Development

If you want to succeed you may need to put in some time learning new techniques and processes to address your weakest business knowledge/skills area.

You will find a range of Small Business Resources with topics relevant to running a small business. See also our business links.
